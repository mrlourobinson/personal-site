- Impressions growing week over week
- Click-through rate >2%
- No mobile usability issues
- No security issues

- `Lou Robinson` - Your site should rank
- `Lou Robinson CNN` - Should be top 3

## 💡 Pro tips

1. **Content is king**: Keep your projects updated - each new project is a new reason for Google to crawl your site

2. **CNN is your superpower**: That backlink is worth more than 100 random links. Make sure it's always active.

3. **Social signals matter**: Regular sharing from your social accounts helps Google understand you're active and relevant

4. **Award mentions**: Add any new awards immediately - they're massive trust signals

5. **Consistency**: Keep your name, title, and bio consistent across all platforms

6. **Mobile-first**: Google indexes mobile version first - your responsive design is perfect

7. **Page speed**: Your SvelteKit setup is already fast - maintain this

8. **Fresh content**: Even small updates signal to Google your site is actively maintained

## 🚨 Common mistakes to avoid

- ❌ Don't stuff keywords unnaturally
- ❌ Don't buy backlinks
- ❌ Don't copy content from other sites
- ❌ Don't change your canonical URLs
- ❌ Don't block important pages in robots.txt
- ❌ Don't forget to update sitemap when adding pages
- ❌ Don't use duplicate meta descriptions
